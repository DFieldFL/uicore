# Heading---docs/pages/heading.mdx

Heading renders H1 to H6 tag based on `level` prop. It supports all [Styled Props](/styled-props).

At this moment, HDL defines four levels of heading (H1 to H4), H5 and H6 are not used.

## Weights

```js live
<Layout.Vertical id="heading-weights">
  <Heading level={1}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
  <Heading level={1} font={{ weight: 'bold' }}>
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
  </Heading>
  <Heading level={1} font={{ weight: 'semi-bold' }}>
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
  </Heading>
  <Heading level={1} font={{ weight: 'light' }}>
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
  </Heading>

  <Heading level={2}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
  <Heading level={2} font={{ weight: 'bold' }}>
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
  </Heading>
  <Heading level={2} font={{ weight: 'semi-bold' }}>
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
  </Heading>
  <Heading level={2} font={{ weight: 'light' }}>
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
  </Heading>

  <Heading level={3}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
  <Heading level={3} font={{ weight: 'bold' }}>
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
  </Heading>
  <Heading level={3} font={{ weight: 'semi-bold' }}>
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
  </Heading>
  <Heading level={3} font={{ weight: 'light' }}>
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
  </Heading>

  <Heading level={4}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
  <Heading level={4} font={{ weight: 'bold' }}>

  <Heading level={4} font={{ weight: 'semi-bold' }}>

</Layout.Vertical>
```

## Render as block

```js live
<Layout.Vertical id="heading-block">
  <Heading level={1}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
  <Heading level={2}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
  <Heading level={3}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
  <Heading level={4}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
</Layout.Vertical>
```


## Intent

Heading supports [Intent](/intent) where an intent color is applied.

```js live
<Layout.Vertical id="heading-intent">
  <Heading intent="primary">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
  <Heading intent="success">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
  <Heading intent="warning">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
  <Heading intent="danger">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
</Layout.Vertical>
```

## Combining with other styled props

```js live=true
<Layout.Vertical id="heading-styled-props">
  <Heading
    padding="small"
    font={{ size: 'large', align: 'center' }}
    border={{ color: 'green500' }}
    background="grey100">
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
  </Heading>
</Layout.Vertical>
```
