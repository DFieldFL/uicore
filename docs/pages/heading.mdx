# Heading---docs/pages/heading.mdx

Heading renders H1 to H6 tag based on `level` prop. It supports all [Styled Props](/styled-props).

At this moment, HDL defines four levels of heading (H1 to H4), H5 and H6 are not used.

## Weights

```js live
<Heading level={1}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading level={1} weight="bold">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading level={1} weight="semi-bold">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading level={1} weight="light">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading level={2}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading level={2} weight="bold">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading level={2} weight="semi-bold">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading level={2} weight="light">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading level={3}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading level={3} weight="bold">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading level={3} weight="semi-bold">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading level={3} weight="light">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading level={4}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading level={4} weight="bold">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading level={4} weight="semi-bold">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading level={4} weight="light">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
```

## Render as block

```js live
<Heading level={1}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading level={2}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading level={3}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading level={4}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
```

## Render as inline

```js live
<Heading level={1} inline>Heading Level 1</Heading>
<Heading level={2} inline>Heading Level 2</Heading>
<Heading level={3} inline>Heading Level 3</Heading>
<Heading level={4} inline>Heading Level 4</Heading>
```

## Intent

Heading supports [Intent](/intent) where an intent color is applied.

```js live
<Heading intent="primary">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading intent="success">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading intent="warning">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading intent="danger">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
```
