## Ellipsis / Text Truncation

When `lineClamp` prop is provided, `Text` will handle string truncation automatically. Note that the truncation
only happens when there is not enough space to render the text content.

### Single line

Single line with fluid width from parent.

```js live=true
<Layout.Horizontal spacing="medium">
  <Text font="normal" color="red500" lineClamp={1}>
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps
    over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The
    quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
  </Text>
  <Text font="normal" color="green500" lineClamp={1}>
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps
    over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The
    quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
  </Text>
  <Text font="normal" color="blue500" lineClamp={1}>
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps
    over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The
    quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
  </Text>
</Layout.Horizontal>
```

Single line with fixed width set from parent.

```js live=true
<Container width={300} flex>
  <Text font="normal" color="red500" lineClamp={1}>
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
  </Text>
  <Text font="normal" color="green500" lineClamp={1}>
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
  </Text>
  <Text font="normal" color="blue500" lineClamp={1}>
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
  </Text>

```

Single line with width set from Text itself.

```js live=true
<Text font="normal" lineClamp={1} width={100}>
  The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
</Text>
```
