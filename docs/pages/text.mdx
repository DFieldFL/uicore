## Ellipsis / Text Truncation

When `lineClamp` prop is provided, `Text` will handle string truncation automatically. Note that the truncation
only happens when there is not enough space to render the text content.

### Single line

```js live=true
<Text font="normal" border padding="small" width={100} lineClamp={1}>
  The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
</Text>
```

### Multiple lines

```js live=true
<Text font="normal" width={200} lineClamp={3}>
  The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps
  over the lazy dog.
</Text>
```

### No tooltip when there is enough space to render the whole text

```js live=true
<Text font="normal" width={300} lineClamp={3}>
  The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps
  over the lazy dog.
</Text>
```
